---

## Most Surprising Finding

The gender gap was much larger than expected, **74.2% of women survived vs only 18.9% of men**.

Also surprising: **only 22.9% of passengers had a recorded Cabin**, meaning most of the data has missing cabin info, which makes it unreliable for any cabin-based analysis.
